Roasted Heirloom Tomato Tart

A celebration of peak-summer produce. Flaky, butter-laced pastry topped with slow-roasted tomatoes, fresh thyme, and a hint of wild honey.

Complexity

Intermediate

Servings

4-6 People

Calories

320 kcal

Ingredients

All-Purpose Flour 250g, chilled and sifted
Unsalted Grass-Fed Butter 150g, cut into 1cm cubes
Ice Water 60ml, as needed
Heirloom Tomatoes 500g, assorted colors, sliced thick
Garlic Confit 4 cloves, smashed into a paste
Fresh Thyme 3 sprigs, leaves stripped
Ricotta di Bufala 200g, drained
Wildflower Honey 1 tbsp, for drizzling
