Ingredients

Serves 2-4

Instructions

1

Prepare the Base

Bring 2 cups of salted water to a boil. Add the rinsed quinoa, reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes. Once cooked, fluff with a fork and fold in the lemon zest and a splash of olive oil.

timer 15 Minute Active Prep
2

Sear the Salmon

Pat salmon fillets dry. Season skin generously with sea salt. Place skin-side down in a medium-hot cast iron skillet with a touch of grape seed oil. Press down gently for 30 seconds to ensure a crisp skin. Sear for 4 minutes until skin is golden and crispy.

3

Finish & Assemble

Flip the salmon and cook for 1-2 minutes more for medium-rare. Blister the broccoli in the same pan. Divide quinoa into bowls, top with salmon and broccoli, and drizzle generously with tahini-miso dressing.
